About Us

The Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering, established in 1987, offers UG and PG programs. The Department offers the undergraduate programme of BTech in Electrical & Electronics Engineering with an intake of 120. The Department has well-qualified, passionate, dedicated faculty and has a well-connected alumni network. With the state-of-the art Laboratory in Power Electronics, Control Systems, Power Systems, Computers and High Voltage & Relay, which caters to the need of all the Electrical & Electronics Engineering students. With an excellent rapport and MoU with industries, the Department is organizes seminars, conferences and workshops on a regular basis to cope up with the recent trends in the technological field.

Facilities

Electrical Machines Laboratory

This laboratory consists of electrical machinery required to conduct DC and Synchronous Machine Laboratory for 4th semester and Transformer & Induction Machine Laboratory for 5th semester students of E&E Engineering.

Analog / Power Electronics Laboratory

This laboratory facilitates Analog Electronic Circuits in 3rd semester and Power Electronics lab in 6th semester of E&E Engineering.

Logic Design / Microcontroller Laboratory

This laboratory deals with Logic Design in 3rd semester and Microcontroller lab in 4th semester of E&E Engineering.

Circuits / Measurements / Controls system

This laboratory accommodates Circuit and Measurements in 5th semester and Linear IC & Control System Laboratory in the 6th semester of E&E Engineering.

High Voltage / Relay / Project

This laboratory facilitates high voltage and relay testing experiment by 7th semester students of E&E Engineering.Students are performing experiment involving 50kV of AC and 70kV of DC voltage.

Computer Laboratory

This laboratory conducts Circuit simulation lab in 4th semester, Electrical CAD laboratory in 6th semester, Power system simulation laboratory in 7th semester of E&E Engineering.

NMAM Institute of Technology

Nitte Mahalinga Adyantaya Memorial Institute of Technology(NMAMIT), Nitte, established in 1986 and recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, has been a constituent college of Nitte (Deemed to be University), Mangaluru,since June 2022. Rank band 101-150 in the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) 2023 by Ministry of Education, Government of India, the College has been placed under ‘Platinum’ category for having high industry linkages by the AICTE-CII Survey of Industry-Linked Technical Institutes 2020. NMAMIT, the off-campus centre of Nitte DU located at Nitte village, has active collaborations with several international universities and organizations for faculty and student exchanges, research, internships and placements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Dive into the realm of electrons and circuits with our FAQ section, demystifying the intricacies of electrical and electronics engineering.

Electrical engineering deals with the study of electricity, while electronics engineering focuses on the study of electronic systems and devices, often involving the control of electrical energy through circuits.

Graduates can pursue careers in various fields, including power systems, telecommunications, electronics design, automation, and control systems, with opportunities in industries such as energy, telecommunications, and manufacturing.

Embedded systems are pivotal in electrical engineering, powering devices and systems with seamless integration of hardware and software. They play a crucial role in automation, control systems, and smart devices, enhancing efficiency and functionality in diverse applications.

Electrical engineers play a crucial role in designing, implementing, and optimizing systems for harnessing renewable energy sources such as solar, wind, and hydro power, contributing to sustainable and clean energy solutions.

Artificial intelligence (AI) intersects with electrical and electronics engineering in areas like machine learning, signal processing, and control systems, enhancing capabilities in automation, data analysis, and decision-making processes within various applications.
